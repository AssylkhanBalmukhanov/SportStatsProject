Underlying Formula

The xG model uses a logistic regression formula to estimate the probability of a shot resulting in a goal:

z = -1.5 - (0.09 * distance) + (0.05 * angle) xG = 1 / (1 + exp(-z))

Where:

  • Distance: The Euclidean distance from the shot location to the center of the goal.
  • Angle: The visible angle of the goal from the shot location.

Headers are assigned a 40% penalty (multiplier of 0.6) to reflect their lower historical conversion rate compared to shots from the foot.
